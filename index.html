About Me

Clint Eastwood is an American actor, director, and producer. He is best known for his roles in western movies such as The Good, the Bad and the Ugly, and for his role as Harry Callahan in the Dirty Harry film series. Eastwood has won numerous awards for his work in film, including four Academy Awards.
